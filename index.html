Design is:

Design is an increasingly fluid idea. What once was a discipline marrying the arts with commerce is now broader than ever. But at its core, design is about both how things work and how they look, move, and feel. Design considers utility and experience, the functional and the emotional, the machine and the human.

I love design because it holds the potential for endless exploration. Each new job, each emergent technology provides fresh challenges, insights, and opportunities.

Ultimately, I love design and creative expression because I believe they are a reflection of our Creator. Our best creative work holds significance not primarily by how it sells a product or service, but that by its sheer nature—its exploration of form and function—it becomes a small reflection of the One whose likeness we mortals bear.
